Multiview playback lets your viewers watch multiple video feeds or camera angles simultaneously, giving them a more immersive viewing experience that can be customized to the event. Multiview is great for sports broadcasting, concerts, and live events to enhance audience engagement.

Multi-Player Approach

Select Assets

Pros

  • Flexible client-side integration
  • No change to encoding workflow
  • Make use of advanced features like DRM, Ads and UI customisation on each player instance

Cons

  • Can't use on Smart TV or CTV devices with single video decoder

Single-Player Approach

Pros

  • Works for all devices, including Smart TV and CTV devices
  • Lower bandwidth with single stream
  • Make use of advanced features like DRM, Ads and UI customisation

Cons

  • Requires additional encoding workflows to create composite streams
